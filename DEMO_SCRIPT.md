# Ward v0 - Ultimate Demo Script
**Duration**: 20 minutes  
**Goal**: Show Ward as an "Intelligence Layer" that integrates with everything.

---

## Pre-Demo Checklist
1. **Clean Slate:** Ensure dashboard isn't cluttered (or use the "Generate Demo Case" button if empty).
2. **Tabs Open:**
   - Landing Page (Platform story)
   - Dashboard (The control tower)
   - Voice Case (The field input)
3. **Context:** "We are not replacing your TMS. We are fixing the chaos your TMS can't see."

---

## ACT 1: The "Plug & Play" Platform (3 mins)
**Screen**: Landing Page

1. **The Hook:**
   - "Logistics chaos happens *outside* your ERP. In phone calls, WhatsApps, and paper documents."
   - "Ward is the AI layer that captures this and structures it."

2. **The Developer Story:**
   - Scroll to **Developers** section.
   - "We are API-first. You can embed Ward into your existing driver apps."
   - Show the code snippet: "It's literally 5 lines of code to start listening for disruptions."

---

## ACT 2: Voice-First Capture (5 mins)
**Screen**: Voice Case Page

1. **The Scenario:**
   - "I'm a driver at JNPT. I don't type. I speak Hindi."

2. **The Action:**
   - Click **"Simulate Demo Input"** (or use Mic if available).
   - *Audio plays:* "Container MSKU... stuck at gate... documents mismatch."
   - Ward transcribes it instantly.

3. **The Clarity Loop:**
   - Ward asks: "What is the container number?" (Text-to-Speech)
   - Click **"Simulate Answer"**: "It is MSKU 123456."
   - **BAM:** Disruption Structured. Type: Customs Hold. Location: JNPT.

---

## ACT 3: The "Deep" Resolution (7 mins)
**Screen**: Case Detail Page

1. **The Handover:**
   - Navigate to the newly created case.
   - Show **Structured Data**: "Ward extracted all this from voice."

   - Switch to **Intelligence** tab.
   - "Let's prove the fix."

   - Show **Discrepancy Alert**: "HS Code mismatch detected."

## ACT 4: Low-Friction Sharing (2 mins)
**Screen**: Case Detail Page header

1. **The Problem:** "Your vendors don't have logins."
2. **The Solution:**
   - Click **"Share Case"** button.
   - "I just generated a secure Magic Link."
   - "The CHA clicks this, sees *only* this case, and uploads the fix. No signup required."

## ACT 5: The Strategic View (3 mins)
**Screen**: Analytics Dashboard

   - "Because Ward structured everything, you now have this."

   - "You can see your 'Mean Time to Resolve' dropping."
